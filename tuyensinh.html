2022 Admission

DANH SÁCH ĐÀO TẠO

Specialities Duration Admissions quota
3D MODELING 2 years 60 triệu
3D ANIMATION 2 years 60 triệu
DIGITAL GRAPHIC DESIGN 2 years 60 triệu
GAME DEVELOPMENT 2 years 60 triệu
FULL-STACK DEVELOPMENT 2 years 60 triệu
FULL-STACK DIGITAL MARKETING 2 years 60 triệu

Who can apply?

High school students who are interested in Information Technology, Design, Digital Marketing and desire to build a solid foundation for their career.
Students of Vocational School, College, and University; individuals who would like to build a career after completing their military service.
Programmers, designers and digital marketers who want to improve their knowledge and strengthen their expertise.

Admission Process

Candidates complete the online application. Contact details (phone, email, etc.) must be provided accurately in order to receive further instruction from Star Classes.

Candidates can visit VTC Academy campus and submit in person VTC Academy Plus Hà Nội: Tầng 5 – Tháp C, Tòa nhà Central Point, 219 Trung Kính, P. Yên Hòa, Q. Cầu Giấy, TP. Hà Nội

After the application is submitted (in either of the two forms above),Star Classes Admission Counselor will contact the applicants to instruct and guide them through the admission process. Admitted candidates will receive admission notice and will be instructed to complete the admission procedure as soon as possible.

Required documents

1. Application form for long-term programs (Star Classes’s form)
2.Admission letter (Sent by VTC Academy to the email of the applicants)
3. Photo/scanned copy of High School Diploma or transcript with GPA 5.0 or higher in 1st semester of 12th grade if the applicant has not graduated from high school
4. Photo/scanned copy of National Identity Card/Citizen Identification Card/Passport
5. Photo of bank transfer confirmation or Tuition receipt
6. Prepare all required documents and send photos/scanned copies to Counseling Department via email: VTC Academy Hanoi: tuyensinh@vtc.edu.vn
